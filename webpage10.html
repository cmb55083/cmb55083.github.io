Common Tips For Dogs

  1. Keep your pets away from stray animals
  2. Take your vet annually to the veterinarian
  3. Groom your pet for flees and tics; they can cause nasty diseases
  4. Never give animals fruits your unsure would harm them
  5. Clean your pet's ears out occasionally
  6. Dont allow dogs to eat chocolate
  7. Feed your pet rice if they have an upset stomach
  8. Treat heartworms annualy
  9. Keep dogs from eating to much grass
  10. Make sure you have a collar or a tracking device for your dog
